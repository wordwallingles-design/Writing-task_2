Writing Task 2 - 8th grades

“In your opinion, why should teenagers include regular exercise in their daily routine, and what benefits do you think are most important for them? Look at this example."

Honestly, I didn't like exercise but after watching a documentary about the importance of exercise, I changed my mind. I think teenagers should exercise every day because it makes life way better. When I work out or play sports now, I feel less stressed and more energetic, so school doesn’t feel as exhausting as before.

It also keeps me healthy and strong, which is important since we spend so much time sitting in class or on our phones.

In addition, exercising with friends is fun—it helps us stay connected and builds confidence. For me, the best benefits are energy, focus, and feeling good about myself.
